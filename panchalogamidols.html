Panchalogam Idols

Panchalogam Idols are sacred Hindu deity statues crafted using a traditional blend of five metals Gold, Silver, Copper, Zinc, and Iron. These metals symbolize the five elements of nature (earth, water, fire, air, and space) and are believed to bring spiritual energy, prosperity, and divine blessings to worshippers.
